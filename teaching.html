by Esteban Ortiz-Ospina and Max Roser[cite]
Our articles and data visualizations rely on work from many different people and organizations. When citing this entry, please also cite the underlying data sources. This entry can be cited as:

Esteban Ortiz-Ospina and Max Roser (2019) - "Teaching Resources". Published online at OurWorldInData.org. Retrieved from: 'https://ourworldindata.org/teaching' [Online Resource]

Welcome to the Our World in Data Teaching Hub. Here you will find resources for teaching and learning about global development – no background knowledge required.

For each of the topics listed below, you will find interactive teaching notes, presentation slides, charts and many other resources.

This page is work in progress, and we will be updating it regularly. If you have comments or suggestions, drop us an email: info@ourworldindata.org
