Ecological impacts of marine plastic debris

by Hannah Ritchie and Max Roser[cite]
Our articles and data visualizations rely on work from many different people and organizations. When citing this entry, please also cite the underlying data sources. This entry can be cited as:

Hannah Ritchie and Max Roser (2018) - "Ecological impacts of marine plastic debris". Published online at OurWorldInData.org. Retrieved from: 'https://ourworldindata.org/ecological-impacts-of-marine-plastic-debris' [Online Resource]
